VISION

OUR STORY

Founded in 2025 by Mahbub Kousar and Mannan Sami, Asymptote Labs is an initiative to research, prototype, and ship groundbreaking tools in AI, software, and community empowerment.

Like an asymptote in mathematics that approaches but never reaches a limit, we believe innovation is an endless journey of getting closer to perfection without ever claiming to have reached it.

WHY "ASYMPTOTE"?

In mathematics, an asymptote is a line that a curve approaches but never quite reaches.

Similarly, our work approaches the ideals of perfect technology without claiming to have reached them. We believe in constant improvement, iteration, and learning.
